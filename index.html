Hi! I am a fourth year PhD student at KU Leuven, advised by Daan Huybrechs.

I do research in numerical analysis at the intersection of numerical linear algebra and approximation theory. In particular, during my PhD I have worked on sampling theory, efficient solvers and applications associated with non-orthogonal expansions. To this end, I use tools from computational harmonic analysis, applied functional analysis and random matrix theory. My favorite basis functions are rational!
